FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from clients before starting professional work.

Do I need to file Income Tax Return if tax is already deducted?

Yes, in many cases return filing may still be required even if TDS has been deducted. Filing depends on income level, type of income, refund claim, foreign assets, capital gains and other applicable conditions.

Can you handle Income Tax Notices and Appeals?

Yes. We assist in reviewing income tax notices, preparing replies, compiling evidence and representing appeal matters before appropriate authorities such as CIT(A) and ITAT.

Is GST registration compulsory for every business?

GST registration depends on turnover, nature of supply, place of supply, e-commerce transactions and other conditions. A proper review is required before deciding whether registration is mandatory.

Do private limited companies need annual ROC compliance?

Yes. Companies are required to comply with ROC filings and other statutory requirements every year, even if there is no business activity, unless the company is legally closed or struck off.

Can you assist with licenses and registrations?

Yes. We provide support for Udyam, IEC, FSSAI, APEDA, Spices Board, BIS, RERA, LEI, Trademark and other business-related registrations based on client requirements.
